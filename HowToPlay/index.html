How To Play

  1. Use the arrow keys to move the player.
  2. Press the spacebar to shoot bullets.
  3. Shoot enemy missiles and prevent them from getting below the screen.
  4. If they cross, you will lose a life.
  5. If you shoot a bullet and kill a missile, you will score a point.
  6. If you shoot a missile, you will score a point.
  7. Collect the supply drops to get another life!
  8. Conserve bullets! Make sure you don't waste them!
  9. Collect the bullet drops to get more bullets!
  10. Keep shooting the missiles and don't let any of them cross off your screen!

Good luck and have fun!
